Why chocolate is dangerous

Dogs metabolize theobromine much more slowly than humans. From about 20mg per kg of body weight, mild symptoms like vomiting appear; over 40mg/kg risks heart problems; over 60mg/kg can cause seizures. The higher the cocoa content (dark, baking), the more dangerous.

If you found them eating it

  • Check the wrapper for type and amount eaten
  • Do NOT force vomiting — let the vet handle it safely
  • The first 1–2 hours are the treatment window
  • Symptoms can appear 6–12 hours later even if none show now

This is a reference estimate and does not replace diagnosis. If ingestion is confirmed, contact a vet first.
