Standard core program

  • 6 weeks: DHPP 1 + corona
  • 8 weeks: DHPP 2 + corona
  • 10 weeks: DHPP 3 + kennel cough
  • 12 weeks: DHPP 4 + kennel cough
  • 14 weeks: DHPP 5 + influenza
  • 16 weeks: influenza + rabies

After the series, an antibody titer check confirms immunity, followed by yearly boosters. Heartworm and flea/tick prevention are given monthly, separately.

Programs vary by clinic and region. Follow your vet's actual schedule.
