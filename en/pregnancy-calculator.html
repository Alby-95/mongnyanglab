Pregnancy length and care

Dogs are pregnant about 63 days (58–68), cats about 65 days (60–70). Pregnancy can be confirmed by ultrasound around week 4, and food should increase gradually from week 6. Set up a quiet whelping box 1–2 weeks before the due date.

Signs labor is near

Body temperature drops about 1°C in the 24 hours before birth, with reduced appetite and nesting behavior. If labor starts but no newborn appears within an hour, or green discharge comes first, contact a vet immediately.
