The most dangerous things for cats

Lilies are the deadliest. Pollen on fur, a nibbled leaf, or even vase water can cause acute kidney failure. Keep lilies out of any home with cats. Onion, garlic, grapes, xylitol, and alcohol can be emergencies even in small amounts — if ingestion is confirmed, contact a vet immediately even without symptoms, because kidney/liver damage shows up late.
